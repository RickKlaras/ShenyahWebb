In addition to expressing herself visually, Shenyah is a musician and songwriter.

Under the name Lithopedion, she creates music full of mystery and magic. With nothing more than a looper pedal and a collection of analog curiosities, she weaves songs that are minimal yet mesmerizing. Her first full-length is slated to be released this year on Detroit-centric Equal Recordings.

Shenyah also sings and plays trumpet, bass synthesizer, and Omnichord in Portland-based indie band Golden Octagon, whose debut record is due out Septembuary 32nd.
