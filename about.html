About Shenyah Webb

Shenyah Webb is a painter, welder, and musician. A Detroit native, she attended The College for Creative Studies, where she focused on industrial design. After completing her studies, she spent time exploring the United States and living in Hawaii, California, and Arizona.

For the past decade, Shenyah has made her home in Portland, Oregon, where she manages a local optical practice and serves as the arts editor for NAILED Magazine.

In her latest series of paintings, she explores her passions for color, design, and the science of optics.
